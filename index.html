International Student Association

The NEUISA (Northeastern University International Student Assistance) is a platform for incoming international students to assist them in their early days assistance. Students can contact the voulunteers from the University for pickup from the airport.

We also help them provide accommodation, provide information about opening of bank accounts, familiarize them with the happenings in and around the university,etc. To book travel plan and accomodation student need to Sign Up first from the link on right corner of this page!!



If you are already a student at Northeastern, you can help us by volunteering.
Mail us at admin-isa@husky.neu.edu

Pick-up from airport

Searching for accomodation
